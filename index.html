Hyunjong Choi

is a Postdoc in the ECE department at UC Riverside. My research interests are real-time embedded systems, cyber–physical systems (CPS), and intelligent autonomous systems including self-driving vehicles, especially the design and implementation of a robust, and predictable scheduling algorithm with an analyzable framework under dynamic timing constraints. I'm a member of Real-Time Embedded and Networked systems Lab (RTEN) at UCR.
