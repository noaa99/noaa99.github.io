HYUNJONG CHOI

is a Ph.D. student in the ECE department at UC Riverside. My research is about real-time embedded system design and cyber-physical systems, specifically building a robust and predictable scheduling algorithm with a analyzable framework under dynamic timing constraints. Now, I'm a member of Real-Time Embedded and Networked systems Lab (RTEN) at UCR.
